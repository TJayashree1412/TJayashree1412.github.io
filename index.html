Pura Ulun Danu Bratan

Hindu temple on Bratan Lake, Bali, Indonesia.

More Info

This temple is also called as Bali temple on the lake because it looks like floating when the Bratan river water rises. Lake Bratan is known as the Lake of Holy Mountain due to the fertility of this area. Located 1200 m above sea level, it has a cold tropical climate.

Positano, Italy

The jewel in the Almafi Coast, is the legendary Italian resort.

More Info

Dramatic, deluxe and more than a little dashing, Positano is the Amalfi Coast's front-cover splash, with vertiginous houses tumbling down to the sea in a cascade of sun-bleached peach, pink and terracotta.
